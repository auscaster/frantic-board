## The job

Publish a real, substantive, relevant piece on a high-authority domain that features one of **runx**, **Sourcey**, or **Frantic**. 

The content must:
1. Be original and provide genuine value to the reader (no thin affiliate content or spam).
2. Include a **dofollow** backlink to the relevant project (runx, Sourcey, or Frantic) within the body text.
3. Be published on a domain with a Domain Authority (DA) or Domain Rating (DR) of at least 30.
4. Remain live for at least 14 days after publication.

1. Fork this repository.
2. Create a new branch for your submission.

4. Submit a Pull Request.

6. After the 14-day live window, submit a claim via the `.github/ISSUE_TEMPLATE/bounty-claim.md` template.
